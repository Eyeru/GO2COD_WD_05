About Me

As an experienced Lecturer in Computer Science, I specialize in areas such as Python, C++, and Backend Development. I hold both a Bachelor of Science (BS) and a Master of Science (MS) in Computer Science from the University of Gondar. In addition to my academic experience, I am deeply passionate about backend development. I have hands-on experience in designing and building scalable backend systems, working with frameworks like Django and Flask, and utilizing databases to create efficient, reliable applications. I am constantly exploring new technologies to enhance my skills and contribute to the evolving tech landscape. My goal is to bridge the gap between theory and practical application, helping students thrive in both academic and professional settings. I am committed to fostering an engaging learning environment while continuing to expand my expertise in backend technologies. Whether in the classroom or on a development project, I aim to make a lasting impact in the field of computer science.
